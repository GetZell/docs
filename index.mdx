## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Bots" icon="robot">
    Create personalized AI prospects with unique personalities, objections, and scenarios. Your team practices with realistic simulations that adapt to their responses.

    [Learn how to create bots →](/guides/bots)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Custom Scorecards" icon="star">
    Define evaluation criteria that matter to your business. Track performance across categories, gather actionable insights, and identify coaching opportunities.

    [Learn how to create scorecards →](/guides/scorecards)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Structured Courses" icon="book-open">
    Build multi-module training programs combining instructional content with practice sessions. Set deadlines, enable rolling enrollment, and monitor completion.

    [Learn how to set up courses →](/guides/courses)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Task Management" icon="list-check">
    Assign recurring or one-time training tasks to individuals or groups. Automate scheduling and track completion rates.

    [Learn how to assign tasks →](/guides/tasks)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team Organization" icon="users">
    Organize team members into groups for targeted training, access control, and progress tracking by department, role, or skill level.

    [Learn how to manage groups →](/guides/groups)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Slack Integration" icon="slack">

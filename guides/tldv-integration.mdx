## Overview

The tl;dv integration lets tenant admins import recent customer-facing tl;dv meeting recordings into Zell. After connecting, you can review recent meetings, import selected meetings manually, and optionally auto-import meetings that match your filters.

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tenant Settings">
    From anywhere in Zell, click your avatar and open **Tenant Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open tl;dv">
    In the Settings sidebar, go to **Integrations** and select **tl;dv**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your API key">
    Paste your tl;dv API key into the **API key** field.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure import filters">
    Choose which meetings should qualify for review and auto-import:

    - **Include title keywords**: only include meetings whose title matches one of these keywords.
    - **Exclude title keywords**: filter out meetings whose title matches one of these keywords.
    - **Minimum duration**: ignore meetings shorter than this many minutes.
    - **Only meetings with external participants**: focus imports on customer-facing conversations.
    - **Auto-import matching meetings**: automatically import meetings that match the saved filters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect tl;dv">
    Click **Connect to tl;dv**. Zell stores the connection and applies the filters you configured.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Review and Import Meetings

Once connected, the tl;dv page shows recent meetings that match your filters by default.

You can:

- Click **Refresh** to reload the recent meeting list.
- Turn on **Show filtered** to see meetings that did not match your filters.
- Select one or more importable meetings.
- Click **Import selected** to import the selected meetings into Zell.

<Note>
  Filtered meetings can still be imported manually when **Show filtered** is enabled, unless they were already imported.
</Note>

## Filtered Meeting Reasons

When a meeting does not match your filters, Zell shows the reason in the meeting table:

| Reason | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Too short | The meeting duration is below your minimum duration. |
| Keyword missing | Include keywords are configured, but the title does not match them. |
| Excluded title | The title matches one of your exclude keywords. |
| Internal only | The meeting does not have external participants. |

## Auto-Import

Auto-import only runs when **Auto-import matching meetings** is enabled. It uses the saved filters on the tl;dv integration page, so update and save the filters whenever your import criteria change.

## Disconnect tl;dv

To disconnect tl;dv, open **Tenant Settings** → **Integrations** → **tl;dv** and click **Disconnect**.

<Warning>
  Disconnecting stops future tl;dv imports. Calls that were already imported into Zell remain available.
</Warning>

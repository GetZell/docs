## Overview

Zell supports multiple languages for both the user interface and AI bot interactions. You can configure language settings to match your team's needs and regional requirements.

## Changing Your Language Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click on your avatar or profile icon in the top-right corner of the application.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Language Settings">
    Select **Settings** → **Language** or **Preferences** → **Language** depending on your interface.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Language">
    Choose your preferred language from the dropdown menu:
    - English
    - German (Deutsch)
    - Italian (Italiano)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply Changes">
    Click **Save** or **Apply** to update your language preference.

    <Check>The interface will reload with your selected language.</Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

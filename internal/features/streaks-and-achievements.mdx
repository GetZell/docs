## Overview

Streaks and achievements motivate reps to keep training and reviewing feedback consistently. They appear around daily review progress, experience rewards, achievement notifications, and streak-related notifications.

## Key Concepts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Daily Progress" icon="calendar-check">
    Progress is tied to actions such as completing Feedback Inbox items.
  </Card>

  <Card title="XP Rewards" icon="badge-check">
    Correct non-passive inbox interactions can award bonus XP.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Achievement Toasts" icon="bell-ring">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Streak Recovery" icon="rotate-ccw">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

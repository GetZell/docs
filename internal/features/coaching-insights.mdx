## Overview

Coaching Insights helps managers understand coaching opportunities across users, signals, calls, scorecards, and snippets. It connects evaluated call moments with actionable feedback and team performance views.

## Key Concepts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Signals" icon="signal">
    Coaching insights are organized around evaluated signals and categories.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Coachable Snippets" icon="message-square-quote">
    Snippets show specific transcript moments with score, coachability, feedback, and replies.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Filters" icon="sliders-horizontal">
    Views can be filtered by users, groups, workspaces, scorecards, source, and date ranges.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manager Scope" icon="sitemap">
    Team-lead views can be constrained by reporting hierarchy and tenant scope.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

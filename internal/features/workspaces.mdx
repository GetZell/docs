## Overview

Workspaces separate users and resources such as bots, scorecards, personas, courses, groups, and calls. They help tenants organize teams, departments, regions, or business units.

## Key Concepts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Isolation" icon="shield">
    Workspace-scoped resources stay separated from other workspaces.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Default Workspace" icon="house">
    Every tenant has a default workspace for initial setup.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Resource Scope" icon="boxes">
    Agents, scorecards, groups, personas, courses, and users are tied to workspaces.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Manager Hierarchy" icon="sitemap">
    Workspace membership and manager relationships influence visibility and reporting.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

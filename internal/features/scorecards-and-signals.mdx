## Overview

Scorecards and signals define how Zell evaluates calls and training interactions. Signals are reusable evaluation criteria; scorecards combine them with weights and assignment rules.

## Key Concepts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Signal" icon="signal">
    A reusable evaluation criterion with detection and scoring configuration.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Scorecard" icon="star">
    A weighted collection of signals assigned to calls, bots, or teams.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Detection Method" icon="search">
    Signals can use LLM rubric, keyword, metric, or semantic detection.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Evaluation Result" icon="clipboard-check">
    Scorecard results feed call analysis, coaching insights, snippets, and reports.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
